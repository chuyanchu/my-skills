# Minimal Academic Style

Use this style for Chinese paper reading reports and research discussion decks.

## Visual DNA

- Background: pure white `#FFFFFF`.
- Title color: deep academic blue `#073B63` or `#0B3A5B`.
- Body text: near black `#242424`.
- Secondary text: dark gray `#555555`.
- Optional emphasis: same deep blue; use sparingly.
- Do not use gradients, shadows, large colored panels, decorative icons, or rounded card layouts.

## Layout

- Default aspect ratio: 16:9 for modern presentations.
- If the user asks for screenshot-like seminar style, 4:3 is acceptable.
- Slide margins: generous left/right margins, around 7-10% of slide width.
- Title: centered near top, large and bold.
- Content: left-aligned under the title.
- Section blocks: use bold section heading, then bullets below.
- Keep whitespace between section blocks; do not use boxes to separate them unless necessary.

## Typography

Recommended Chinese fonts:

- Microsoft YaHei
- PingFang SC
- Noto Sans CJK SC
- Source Han Sans SC

Recommended English fallback:

- Arial
- Helvetica

Typical sizes for 16:9:

- Title: 34-42 pt, bold.
- Section heading: 19-24 pt, bold.
- Body bullets: 18-23 pt.
- Footer/citation: 9-11 pt.

For 4:3:

- Title: 32-38 pt.
- Section heading: 18-23 pt.
- Body bullets: 17-22 pt.

Never shrink body text below 16 pt for a main slide. Split content instead.

## Bullet Style

- Use simple dot bullets.
- Keep bullets concise and action-oriented.
- Avoid nested bullets unless a technical distinction would be lost.
- Prefer parallel structure:
  - "解决什么问题"
  - "为什么旧方法不够"
  - "作者怎么做"
  - "实验证明了什么"

## Common Slide Patterns

### Three Section Notes

Use for experiment ideas or research directions:

```text
Title

实验1：双 agent 协作决策
• Agent A ...
• Agent B ...
• 对比基线 ...

实验2：Skill 冲突与协调
• ...

实验3：Skill 迁移与传播
• ...
```

### Problem-Gap-Method

Use for motivation slides:

```text
Title

问题：...
已有方法不足：...
本文思路：...
```

### Compact Comparison

Use for 3-5 papers:

```text
Title

Paper A：框架型贡献，适合 ...
Paper B：SOP 型贡献，适合 ...
Paper C：失败分析型贡献，适合 ...

结论：...
```

## Anti-Patterns

- Big hero imagery for paper reading reports.
- Decorative cards around every bullet group.
- Dense paragraphs copied from the paper.
- Full citation blocks on main slides.
- Mixed font sizes without hierarchy.
- Red spell-check underlines or unreviewed spelling artifacts.
- Tables with more than 5 columns unless moved to appendix.
